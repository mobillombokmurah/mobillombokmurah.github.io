Creature & Machine

Recently I’ve been making music under the name Creature & Machine. My music blends organic and electronic sounds, and I enjoy experimenting with new ways of performing live. I also make generative visuals from my music using the Processing framework. You can find my recent music on streaming platforms and on Instagram.

Watch this space for new music coming in Winter 2020.
